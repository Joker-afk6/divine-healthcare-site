Who we are

We show up for the people we work with. Every time.

Divine Home Health Care is based at 220 Davidson Avenue in Somerset, NJ. We work with adults with developmental or physical disabilities, helping them build the day-to-day skills that make independent living real.

The practical side looks like cooking, budgeting, getting around town, knowing how to handle a doctor's appointment. The less obvious part is just being consistent. A lot of our clients have had programs come and go. We try to be the ones who stick around.

"Our goal is for every person we support to be able to use the resources around them and feel like a full member of their community."
  • Trained staff, screened and matched to each client
  • Individual care plans that actually change as life changes
  • Direct communication with families throughout
  • Medicaid accepted, with help navigating the paperwork

Our programs

Life outside four walls.

Programs built around real activity, not structured exercises in a room. Here's what a typical week actually looks like.

City parks and outdoor time

Regular outings for fresh air and walking. Simple, but it matters more than most things we do.

Bowling leagues

A recurring favorite. Good for coordination, socializing, and just having a good time with other people.

Gardening

Planting and tending to things. Clients find it grounding in more ways than one.

Cooking and meal prep

Planning a meal, shopping for it, making it. One of the most practical skills we work on.

Computer skills

Email, job searching, online banking, basic internet use. Table stakes for independent life.

Community outings

Grocery stores, pharmacies, libraries, transit. Using the resources that are already out there.

Money management

Budgeting, bank accounts, making change. Small skills with big consequences for real life.

Social skills

Starting conversations, joining groups, spending time with others comfortably. We work on these directly.

Common questions

Things people ask us first.

Do you accept Medicaid?

Yes. Most of our services are covered through New Jersey Medicaid programs, including DDD-funded supports. We'll walk you through what's available and help with the paperwork.

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Somerset, NJ and serve the surrounding counties. Give us a call and we'll give you a quick answer about your specific area.

What if a client's needs change over time?

We revisit care plans regularly and adjust as things change. A plan that made sense at the start of the year may not work by the end of it. We expect that and plan for it.

How do you hire and screen your staff?

Background checks, reference checks, and training specific to supporting adults with disabilities. We don't place someone with a client until we're confident they're a genuine match.

Do you stay in contact with families?

Always. You'll know what's being worked on, how things are going, and where to reach us if something comes up. That's not optional for us.

How quickly can services start?

It depends on the situation, but we move as fast as the proper assessment allows. We won't rush the fit to get someone placed. Call us and we'll give you a realistic timeline.

Ready to find out if we're the right fit?

We don't do pressure or sales pitches. Just an honest conversation about what your situation looks like and whether we can actually help.
